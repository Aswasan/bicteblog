BICTE (Bachelor of Information and Communication Technology in Education) is an undergraduate program in Nepal designed to integrate Information and Communication Technology (ICT) into the field of education. It aims to equip students with a solid foundation in both Education and ICT, fostering innovative teaching and learning practices through technology.
